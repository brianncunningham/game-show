# GameShow Pi — Wi-Fi Setup (Away from Home)

1. Look for **`GameShow-Setup`** in Wi-Fi networks on your phone
2. Connect to it (no password)
3. Open browser → **`http://192.168.4.1:8080`**
4. Tap your network name under "Saved Networks" or enter SSID + password manually
5. Page will say "Tearing down AP..." — **this is normal**, the page will go dead
6. Wait ~15 seconds, reconnect your phone to your normal Wi-Fi
7. Pi is now connected ✅
